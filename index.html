About CFC x Soho House

Creative Futures Collective

Founded in 2019 by Jai Al-Attas & Markie Bryant, the mission of CFC is to unearth and empower the next generation of creative leaders from disenfranchised & impacted communities.
creativefuturescollective.com

Soho House Mentorship Program

Soho Mentorships were set up in 2018 as a 16-week program that connects Soho House members with young people in their city. The purpose is to grow connections, confidence and experience - providing a route for them to access their chosen creative field. Each mentee is given a Soho House membership to help grow their network, and members who take part will receive training on how to deliver advice and coaching.
